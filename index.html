Frequently Asked Questions

See our most asked questions below

Can I track my order?

Yes, you can track your order by clicking on the tracking link in your email or by following the instructions below.

  1. Go to Tracking Page
  2. Enter your order number and email address
  3. Click on the Track Order button to view the status of your order

If you encounter issues please email us at help@anycompany.com

How do I return something?

You can see our return policy and instructions below.

  • Items must be in original condition.
  • All original packaging and materials must be included.
  • Proof of purchase must accompany the return.

To initiate a return please email us at support@anycompany.com
